The Year of Camp-ish

Not Quite Camp… But Definitely Fiesta

No cabins? No problem. This year, we turned a hotel into Camp Fiesta — complete with PJ parties (with the most intense game of musical chairs in camp history), massive donuts, and our first-ever Family Feud (now a tradition, obviously). Even the new campers who’ve never been to actual camp are obsessed — proof that we really are the Greatest Camp in the History of the World. Chaos? Yes. Mishaps? Plenty. Fun? Off the charts. Camp isn't a place — it's us.
